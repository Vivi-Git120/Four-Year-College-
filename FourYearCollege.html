George Mason University

Program of Studies

George Mason University offers a variety of undergraduate, graduate, and professional programs. The university is known for its strong programs in public policy, law, economics, and information technology.

Location

George Mason University is located in Fairfax, Virginia, just a few miles from Washington, D.C. The university's proximity to the nation's capital provides students with unique opportunities for internships, research, and networking.

Tuition

The tuition for in-state students is approximately $12,564 per year, while out-of-state students pay around $36,024 per year. Financial aid and scholarships are available to help offset the cost of tuition.
